TheGuestbook

The Anti-Social Network

We're a different kind of social network. Whereas other networks aim to more and more associate everything you do with your real-life identity forever, TheGuestbook goes the completely opposite direction. Nothing on TheGuestbook can ever be traced back to you, because on this network there is no such thing as an account: everything is strictly anonymous. We consider ourselves the antithesis of permanent records.

Because everything you do on TheGuestbook is anonymous, it fosters sincere discussions where one can truly express his own opinion. To better encourage this goal of truly free expression, we do away with any kind of point system found on similar, more popular websites. On TheGuestbook, everyone has a right to express their opinion without restriction.

But, unlike yet another similar website founded on the principal of anonymity , the users also have a duty to respect the rights of others. If no one respected the rights of others by following their duties, no one would have rights in the first place. For these reasons, TheGuestbook as a community is called to adhere the following rules:

  • Attempting to reveal another's personal identity is strictly prohibited
  • Refrain from any and all illegal activity
  • Do not participate or engage in a flame war
  • Duplicate posting is not allowed
  • Trolling is annoying and outlawed
  • Keep it classy
  • Don't be stupid
